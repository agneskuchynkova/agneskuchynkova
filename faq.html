Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer grind options?

We recommend whole bean for freshness, but we can grind for most brew methods.

When do you ship?

We roast and ship Monday/Thursday. Orders placed by 10am ET roast the same day.

How should I store coffee?

Keep sealed at room temp away from light. No fridge needed.
