Brew Guides

Dial in flavor with our simple, repeatable recipes. Start here and tweak to taste.

Pour-over (V60 / Kalita)
  • Ratio: 1:16 (25g coffee : 400g water)
  • Grind: medium-fine (table salt)
  • Water: 205°F / 96°C
  • Time: 2:45–3:15
French Press
  • Ratio: 1:15 (30g : 450g)
  • Grind: coarse (sea salt)
  • Water: 205°F / 96°C
  • Time: 4:00 (stir and plunge)
AeroPress
  • 17g coffee · 250g water
  • Grind: medium-fine
  • Method: inverted, 1:00 steep, 0:30 press
Espresso
  • 18g in · 36–40g out · 25–30s
  • Adjust grind to hit time and balance.
